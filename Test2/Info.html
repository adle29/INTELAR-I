How to use Intelar ?

Intelar is a prototype of an artificial intelligence still in development designed to impulse self-learning. Intelar has different functions. You can chat with him, ask him about information, and use his integrated graphing calculator. To communicate with him just, type or press the microphone icon on the textfield.

MOOD

To change Intelar's mood, color and tone of voice just tell him what you feel. Example: I love you, I hate you, I like you, I dislike you, etc.

SEARCH FUNCTION

Intelar has an integrated search function. I you ask about a topic, Intelar will tell you a brief explanation about it. You just have to say about "topic". If intelar doesn't find information about what you are requesting you should check if you misspelled a word, or try giving a different name. Examples: For the word "Sports", Intelar won't find anything, but for the word "Sport", Intelar will tell you what he knows. Also "Olympics" doesn't match Intelar's data base but "Olympic games" does.

Commands
  • my name is "your name": so Intelar remembers your name
  • graphing calculator: graphing calculator
  • about "text": search information
  • no more, stop : stop saying information
