How to use Intelar ?

Intelar is a prototype of an artificial intelligence still in development designed to impulse self-learning. Intelar has different functions. You can chat with him, ask him about information, and use his integrated graphing calculator. To communicate with him just, type or press the microphone icon on the textfield.

Commands

  • numbers: graphing calculator
  • about "text": search information
  • Say a sum of numbers
